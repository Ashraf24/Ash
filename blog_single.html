One of the most important lessons you learn on any design course, is that presentation is half the battle. You can produce awe-inspiring design work, but if you can’t present it well to clients, it will be dismissed prematurely. There was a time, when designers spent weeks painstakingly photographing, printing, and mounting their portfolios in order to achieve the right look. Thankfully, these days, designers have an alternative; and we’re delighted that our sister-site, MightyDeals.com, has arranged this huge deal on a huge bundle of 160 Photoshop mockups to perfect your portfolio. These incredible smart files take your flat design work and magically transpose it onto pre-shot real-world objects; giving the impression of a professionally staged and photographed arrangement, every time. Comprising of all manner of print and digital possibilities, including books, magazines, devices, flyers, stationery and a lot more; this bumper collection is enough to keep even the most prolific designer’s portfolio looking fresh for months, perhaps years, to come.
